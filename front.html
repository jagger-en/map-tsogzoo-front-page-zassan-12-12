Information about the town of Vác

Flag of Vác

Coat of arms

  • Continent: Europe
  • Country: Hungary
  • County: Pest
  • Population: Approximately 35,000 inhabitants
  • Total area: 61.60 km2.

  • Geography

    • Vác is located 35 kilometres north of Budapest on the eastern bank of the Danube river, below the bend where the river changes course and flows south. The town is seated at the foot of the Naszály Mountain in the foothills of the Carpathians.

  • Short history

    • The town of Vác dates back almost a thousand years. Due to the favorable natural conditions, the area has naturally been continuously inhabited for thousands of years. The hills, the forest, and the river are good opportunities for obtaining food, creating safe places to live, water transportation, the possibility of crossing the Danube, and the intersection of routes are a good background for trade.   • Tourism

    • Many tourists visit Vác every year, mainly because of its easy accessibility, rich history, and many attractions. Hikers should take a walk on the main square, on the banks of the Danube. Hungry tourists can choose from dozens of restaurants in the city center.
